Our Story

A journey of flavor, frozen in time.

Welcome to Pop Paradise

Welcome to MEMORICE, the popsicle paradise that tantalizes your taste buds with over 50 delectable flavors! At MEMORICE, we take the art of crafting popsicles to a whole new level, offering an irresistible array of frozen treats to satisfy every palate. From classic favorites like creamy vanilla bean and succulent strawberry to exotic delights such as mango chili lime and lavender honey, we have a flavor for everyone's preference.

Quality You Can Taste

What sets MEMORICE apart is not just the sheer variety but also the quality of ingredients we use. We source only the finest, freshest fruits, herbs, and dairy to create popsicles bursting with natural flavors. Each popsicle is a delightful blend of sweet and refreshing goodness, made with love and care to ensure a memorable experience with every lick.

For Every Lifestyle

At MEMORICE, we believe in catering to all tastes and dietary needs. Our menu includes an assortment of vegan, gluten-free, and sugar-free options, ensuring that no one misses out on the frozen joy we have to offer. Whether you're strolling down the boardwalk on a scorching summer day or hosting a special event, MEMORICE popsicles are the perfect companion.

A Feast for the Eyes

Not only are the flavors diverse, but our popsicles also come in an array of eye-catching colors and playful shapes, making them an Instagram-worthy treat that's as visually appealing as it is delicious. We occasionally introduce limited-edition seasonal flavors, so there's always something new and exciting to discover.

Experience the Bliss

Step into our popsicle wonderland, and let your taste buds embark on a journey of unforgettable flavors. With MEMORICE, you'll discover the true essence of summertime bliss in every frozen bite!
